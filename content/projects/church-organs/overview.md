# Overview

The archive should begin with instrument survey notes rather than restoration fantasies.

## First-pass survey topics

- action type and console arrangement
- pipe divisions and obvious tonal layout
- blower and wind system notes
- visible later electrical or control modifications
- access constraints, maintenance condition, and obvious risks

## Immediate next steps

- identify candidate instruments to document
- establish a consistent survey template
- capture photos and terminology notes alongside each survey
