# Church organs

This project collects technical notes, observations, and restoration thinking around church organs as real engineered systems rather than just musical artifacts.

## Focus

- organ action, pipework, wind, and console layout
- condition notes and maintenance observations
- electrical additions or alterations in later eras
- historical context that affects repair choices

## Why archive it

Organs combine craft, mechanics, acoustics, and architecture. Good documentation needs to preserve those layers together.
