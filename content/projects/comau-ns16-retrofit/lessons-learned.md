# Lessons learned

This page starts empty on purpose. It should hold the distilled knowledge that remains true after experiments, rewiring, and false starts have been absorbed.

## Candidate lesson areas

- how to audit a legacy industrial robot before touching control hardware
- what matters most when mapping old actuators, brakes, and feedback devices
- where stepper retrofits are viable and where they are not
- how to use AI productively without weakening engineering discipline
